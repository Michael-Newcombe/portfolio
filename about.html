About Me

Hello! my name is Michael Newcombe, I am a recent Creative Computing graduate from Goldsmiths, University of London where I achieved a First Class Honours. My passion is being able to express my creativity through computing by thinking outside of the box to come up with innovative solutions to problems. I enjoy combining, my technical knowledge and creative side, to create unique pieces of work. For example, for my 3rd-year university project, I developed a mobile web app designed for visually impaired users, that uses machine learning to generate audio descriptions of the user's surroundings. I have always been a creative person, therefore being able to bring my ideas to life through computing is something I enjoy very much. Studying Creative Computing at Goldsmiths has allowed me to do this, by equipping me with the technical knowledge needed, while still allowing me the freedom to express myself. I also have experience in the IT sector as I have worked as a Front End Web Developer Intern for a company called Tedbree Digital and as a 1st Line, IT Support Apprentice for a company called Biotronics3D. If you find some of my work interesting and would like to collaborate on a project, please feel free to contact me. I would love to hear from you.
